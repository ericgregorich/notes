A digital garden is the idea that you work on your notes and thoughts and tend to them over time to grow them (or kill them) as needed. These notes are often shared publicly.

I feel like the traditional "blog" has become a place where we share more polished content, but the garden is where you publish anything regardless. It is much less formal.

A Digital Garden also tends to be less hierarchical, and the notes are more connected. It is intended to more closely represent how our brain works.

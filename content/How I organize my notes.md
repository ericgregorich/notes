## Folders
Folders represent boundaries. I have a folder for Drafts, Blog, Canvas, Tags. My actual notes are in the root folder. 

One of many advantages to having all of my public notes the root folder is that the URL is consistent. If I were to use folders to organize and I were to move a note, the link would break.
## Tags
I try not to overdo tags. I use them only to show a list of notes related to some category. Examples include People, Quotes, SOPs, and Books. 

## Links
I use links for people, places, and things. I also link related notes. Ideally, the link is embedded in the note with clear context given for why the link exists. 

I’ll also link to references and external resources.

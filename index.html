What is a Folgezettel?

Mar 2, 2024

A Folgezettel is a method for organizing notes by providing each note a unique alphanumeric ID.

The main point of a Folgezzetel is to force you to think about the relationships between your notes. Sure, you can simply link between them, but the Fogezzetel also gives you a visual representation of how your ideas may be related.
